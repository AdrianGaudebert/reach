What is this?

Reach is a turn-based, strategy game for two players. Drop your units on the map and make them reach the ennemy line!

How to play

This game is to be played by two players on the same computer. One after the other, each player must drop one and only one unit on the map. After that, all units of that player will move one cell forward if possible, and then attack all ennemy units they can reach.
